# Axiom 2 — Pathways bundle

A generative pathway that delivers one extreme tends to deliver a correlated syndrome of other
traits — including traits that appear orthogonal to the first in the general population.

The bundling lives in the *mechanism*, not in the marginal distributions. Two traits can look
entirely unrelated when you measure them across everyone, because the pathway that ties them
together is only running for a small, extreme subset. The relationship is real but local to
that subset.
